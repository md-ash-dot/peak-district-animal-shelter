What we do?

Animal Rescue

We carry out rescue operations to protect lost, injured, abandoned, abused and stray animals. We want to give all of them a second chance on life through our tireless efforts and compassion. We have a team of highly trained rescue officers, who are equipped to tackle all sorts of situations. Our Animal rescue ambulance is equipped with the best eqipment to attend to all dire situations. And our in house veterinarian hospital is equipped to perform even very complex medical procedures.

Shelter and care

We provide shelter to these wonderful animals through our ever growing infrastructure and dedicated team and volunteers. We provide the best care to all our animals by housing, feeding, nurturing and treating them. We have an amazing team of veterinarians, who work relentlessly everyday around the clock to give them the best care they all deserve. We our cureently equipped to house most types of animals, and always strive to meet individual needs in the best possible way.

Adoption services

We are always putting in our best efforts to finding forever homes and foster homes for all our animals. We believe in vetting all our applicants through an extensive process. We also educate and train them with all the knowledge, skills and equipment needed to give the best possible care to these lovely animals. We encourge everyone to play a part in changing the live's of these wonderful animals by giving or finding them a forever home.
